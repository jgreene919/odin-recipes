Homemade Peanut Butter

Description

Easy, cheap, and endlessly customizable to your individual palette. Better than any of that pathetic crap grocery stores peddle.

Ingredients

  • Food processor
  • Roasted and unsalted peanuts (VERY important that they are unsalted.)
  • (optional) Small spoonful of brown sugar
  • (optional but highly recommended) a spoonful of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add peanuts to food processor
  2. Add other ingredients (if using).
  3. Pulse a few times, then set it to low.
  4. Once it forms a vague sludge, crank it up to high.
  5. At some point it'll turn into a ball. Keep it going.
  6. You need to blend it longer than you think. Blend until it reaches a buttery, spreadable consistency. To test, stop it and scoop out a little bit.
  7. Store in a mason jar in the fridge. Enjoy!

For use with Banana Strawberry Smoothie or Overnight Oats.
