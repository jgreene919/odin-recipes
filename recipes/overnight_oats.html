Overnight Oats

Description

I have made this for breakfast nearly every morning for over two years. Simple and healthy. Especially for runners.

Ingredients

  • Mason jar
  • 0.5 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 tbsp hemp hearts
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 0.25 tsp ground turmeric
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • A big scoop of peanut butter
  • 0.75 cup unsweet almond milk

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to mason jar.
  2. Close the lid and shake vigorously.
  3. Place in fridge overnight.
  4. Put it in a bowl the next morning and reheat in the microwave. Enjoy!
