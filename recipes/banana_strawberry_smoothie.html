Banana Strawberry Smoothie

Description

I hate bananas. Can't get over their texture. However I am a marathon runner, and everything I read about nutrition speaks of their benefits. So I've chosen to blend them up in smoothies instead of eating them straight-up.

Strawberries are great for you too. Everyone should get at least two servings of berries each day, and this should cover both. You can also add in a half cup of spinach, and it just disappears right in. Boom, there's your daily serving of spinach bitch. Didn't even notice it.

Ingredients

  • Blender
  • 1 banana (unfrozen)
  • 1 cup of frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup of almond milk (with more at the ready)
  • 0.5 cup frozen spinach
  • (optional) A big scoop of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Pour in almond milk.
  2. Peel banana and break into chunks. Drop em in your blender.
  3. Add the rest of your ingredients
  4. Pulse it a few times to get a rhythm going, then blend those fucking sons of bitches
  5. Pour in a cup with a straw and enjoy!
