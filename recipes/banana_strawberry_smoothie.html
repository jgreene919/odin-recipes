Banana Strawberry Smoothie

Description

I fucking hate bananas. Can't get over their texture. However I am a vegan marathon runner, and everything I read about nutrition speaks of their benefits. So I've chosen to blend them up in smoothies instead of eating them straight-up.

Strawberries are great for you too. Everyone should get at least two servings of berries each day, and this should cover both. You can also add in a half cup of spinach, and it just disappears right in. Boom, there's your daily serving of spinach bitch. Didn't even notice it.

I recommend using frozen strawberries and unfrozen bananas. If both are frozen the smoothie becomes too thick.

    • Blender
      1 banana (unfrozen)
      1 cup of frozen strawberries
      1 cup of almond milk (with more at the ready)
      0.5 cup frozen spinach
      (optional) A big scoop of peanut butter
    • Pour in almond milk.
      Peel banana and break into chunks. Drop em in your blender.
      Add the rest of your ingredients
      Pulse it a few times to get a rhythm going, then blend those fucking sons of bitches
      Pour in a cup with a straw and enjoy!
